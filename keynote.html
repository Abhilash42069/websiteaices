KEYNOTE SPEAKERS

Dr. Humaira Nisar
University of Tunku Abdul Rahman, Malaysia

Humaira Nisar received the BE (Honors) in Electrical Engineering from University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore, Pakistan. She received MS degree in Nuclear Engineering from Quaid-e-Azam University, Islamabad, Pakistan. She received MS degree in Mechatronics and Ph.D. in Information and Mechatronics from Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology, Republic of Korea. Currently, she is Associate Professor at Department of Electronic Engineering, Universiti Tunku Abdul Rahman, Malaysia. She is also senior member of IEEE. Her research interests include signal and image processing, bio medical imaging, brain signal analysis, motion estimation and video compression.

Dr. Neeraj Sharma
IIT BHU

Neeraj Sharma is currently a Professor with the School of Biomedical Engineering, IIT (BHU), Varanasi. His current research interests include biomedical image processing, bioinstrumentation, and artificial intelligence. Some of his publications topics are as follows : biosensors,brain,medical image processing,tumours,biomedical MRI,cellular biophysics,image segmentation,molecular biophysics,II-VI semiconductors,adhesion,cancer,learning (artificial intelligence),neurophysiology,zinc compounds,MIM structures,Unified Modeling Language,alumina,bioelectric phenomena,biomechanics, biomedical electrodes,biomedical materials,cell motility,chemical sensors,coatings,convolutional neural nets
