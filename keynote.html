KEYNOTE SPEAKERS

Prof. Satish K. Sharma
Professor and Director, San Diego State University

Prof. Satish Kumar Sharma is currently a Professor and the Director with the Antenna and Microwave Laboratory, San Diego State University. He has authored or co-authored over 200 research papers published in international journals and conferences. He has co-edited three books. He holds one U.S. and one Canadian patents. Dr. Sharma is a Full Member and the Chair of the technical activities of the USNC/URSI, Commission B, Fields and Waves. He received the IEEE AP-S Harold A. Wheeler Prize Paper Award in 2015, the National Science Foundation’s Prestigious Faculty Early Development (CAREER) Award in 2009, and the Young Scientist Award of URSI Commission B, Field and Waves, during the URSI Triennial International Symposium on Electromagnetic Theory, Pisa, Italy, in 2004. He was recognized as the Outstanding Associate Editor (AE) of the IEEE Transactions on Antennas and Propagation (IEEE TAP) in 2014. He served as an AE for IEEE TAP (2009-2017) and currently serves as an AE for the IEEE Antennas and Wireless Propagation Letters.
